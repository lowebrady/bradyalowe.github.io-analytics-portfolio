05 — MILESTONE PROJECT

Allbirds A/B Email Campaign Analysis

Conducted and analyzed an A/B test on promotional email subject lines for a seasonal marketing campaign. Evaluated open rates, click-through rates, and conversion metrics to provide data-backed recommendations for improving customer engagement and campaign ROI.

Finding: Curiosity-gap subject lines outperformed discount-forward lines by 22% on click-through rate, despite similar open rates — suggesting Allbirds' audience responds more to brand voice than price signals. Recommendation: Shift email creative strategy toward narrative-led subject lines and reserve discount messaging for cart-abandonment sequences.
